Our story

Computer Doctor was set up in 2000 specializing in repairing, upgrading, sales, training, and networking computers.

In 2002, we moved to a new premise in Coolock Dublin 17 employing 6 staff, were we specialized in going to Home and Business and resolving their issues.

In 2005/2006, we had our own slot “ask the computer doctor” on Newstalk with Damien Kyberd, we did 27 shows. We have also been on The Gerry Ryan, George Hook, Colm Hayes, Ryan Tubirty, Q102, 98fm, Fm104 and Talk Back Radio.

In 2012, we moved to a new premise in Fairview Dublin 3, the premise was the old Permanent TSB Bank on Edge’s Corner. We bought out Marx-Computers, talking all their customers over.
